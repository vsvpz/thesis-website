This experiment began as an in-class photography workshop at Otis College of Art & Design in the MFA Graphic Design program. We were asked to bring in 10 objects that we would be using to create photograms (a method of photography-based image-making that was unkown to me). I decided to pick up small trinkets and jewellery - chachkis, if you will - around my apartment that were of sentimental value to me. From a ring my grandmother gave me to small ceramic frogs my aunt had sent me off with as a good luck charm, I had no idea what kind of images I would be making.

photogram

A photogram is a photographic print made by laying objects onto photographic paper and exposing it to light.

As defined by the Tate Modern.
